Welcome to Jodhpur, The Blue City

Jodhpur, known as the Blue City, is a vibrant destination in Rajasthan, famous for its stunning blue-painted houses, majestic forts, and rich cultural heritage. Nestled in the Thar Desert, this royal city offers a blend of history, architecture, and warm hospitality.
