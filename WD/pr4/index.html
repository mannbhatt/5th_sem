Government Engineering College, Gandhinagar

Government Engineering College, Gandhinagar (GECG) is one of the premier engineering institutions in Gujarat. The college is dedicated to providing quality technical education, innovation, and research in various fields of engineering.

Established with the vision of producing skilled technocrats, GECG offers a range of undergraduate programs under Gujarat Technological University (GTU).
